About

Overview

-Personalized Intelligence-

L.U.X.E. is a platform built around "AERIS" that connects living spaces, mobility, and online experiences into one continuous journey. Whether you are at home, school, work, or on the move, the same companion quality and UI stay by your side.

User information and the persona of AERIS are stored in a dedicated encrypted cloud assigned to each individual. It is fully isolated from L.U.X.E.'s shared servers, and every device reproduces the same experience through this "personal cloud" from anywhere.

Focus areas: AI devices / web applications / interactive experiences

Engagement model: end-to-end from consulting and PoC to production operations

Technology stack: Edge x Cloud architecture / low-latency UI / secure synchronization

OUR AI

AERIS

A cloud-connected AI partner that balances personalization and safety.

AERIS learns every individual's rhythm, routines, and preferences to work as a companion AI that disappears into daily life. The same persona lives inside every L.U.X.E. device, whether at home, at work, or on the move, so the "AERIS you know" always answers.

Learning and automation always happen with your permission and control. AERIS may anticipate needs behind the scenes, but the final decision stays with the user by design.

ON-DEVICE LEARNING

Learning runs directly on the device where AERIS is operating.

Behavior logs and interaction history are transformed into the minimum data required for learning and saved only inside an encrypted, user-dedicated cloud vault that even our shared servers cannot read.

That cloud data is treated as personal information, with access limited to the user and AERIS. AI-based anomaly detection plus our 24/7 security engineers monitor for suspicious activity and instantly cut off and investigate anything abnormal.

PERSONA SYNC

Encrypted personas stay in sync safely.

Your AERIS persona lives in a dedicated encrypted cloud so the same companion responds from smart homes, PCs, mobiles, or in-vehicle devices, no matter where you are.

Each persona is logically isolated per user and never mixed with other users or our operations data. Adding devices doesn't multiply companions; it lets the same AERIS work seamlessly across endpoints.

PERMISSION-FIRST

Consent, then action - always revocable.

New automations or service connections are off by default and explained on screen before being enabled. They only run after the user explicitly agrees.

Any rule or integration can be paused or deleted with a single tap, and nothing turns on automatically without user action.

EXPLAINABLE

Every action keeps its reasoning and history.

Each recommendation or automation links to the "why" and "source data," which you can review later. Users can trace what inputs AERIS referenced and why it acted at a given timing.

Logs are viewable chronologically in the user console, and any entries can be bulk-deleted. Feedback feeds straight back into AERIS so its behavior updates immediately.

Vision

Toward a world where AI feels natural.

L.U.X.E. is building a platform with AERIS at its core to smoothly unite living spaces, mobility, and online experiences.

No matter if you are at home, at school, at work, or in the city, the same AERIS persona quietly tunes the environment so people can live without consciously thinking about technology. That is our vision.

Our headquarters is designed as a "prototype" of that future city, with the entire building embodying the L.U.X.E. worldview and experience concept.

HEADQUARTERS

A step ahead.

The L.U.X.E. headquarters is more than an office; it is an experimental space for "AI-native" urban planning.

Floor-to-ceiling glass facades and gently curved floors connect workspaces, lounges, cafes, and meeting areas so employees and guests can keep conversations flowing without thinking about location.

Environmental controls, security, guidance, and collaboration tools all link with AERIS, letting the entire building behave like one L.U.X.E. device. The experiences tested here become blueprints for future homes, offices, and city projects.

URBAN EXPERIENCE

A city where L.U.X.E. feels second nature.

LUXE contacts, glasses, smart locks, and mobility-enabled devices quietly operate across the city.

From commuting navigation and in-store check-ins to access control at home or work, AERIS stitches everything together so the flow of the day itself becomes an optimized experience.

Technology stays in the background and the UI surfaces only when needed. Rather than thinking "I used LUXE," people simply feel "I was effortlessly supported all day" - that is the standard we pursue for future cities.

SECURITY & OPERATIONS

24/7 security operations.

L.U.X.E. combines AERIS detection with human judgment to run a continuous monitoring posture. Access logs from urban infrastructure, home devices, and cloud services are analyzed across systems to spot anomalies early.

When an incident is suspected, privileges and policies automatically shrink the blast radius, then on-call security engineers-available around the clock-investigate, decide, and recover.

The entire operation is built on the idea of containing issues before they happen, not scrambling after the fact.
