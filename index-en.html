Our AI

AERIS

AERIS is a companion AI that studies daily behavior, habits, and personality to become uniquely yours. The same “you-only AERIS” syncs across every L.U.X.E. device, executes routines with your permission, and anticipates the next best step wherever you are.

  • Personal by default： On-device learning keeps every experience personal
  • Persona Sync： Securely sync encrypted personas across devices
  • Permission-first： Permission-first actions, revocable at any time
  • Explainable： Explainable decisions with traceable histories
01

Product Scope

We design AI devices that link everyday life, the home, and the city.

AERIS sits at the core to sense the environment, decide, and automate with explicit permission.

User data and each AERIS persona live inside a dedicated encrypted cloud, synchronized safely across every endpoint.

All information stays isolated from L.U.X.E. servers or clouds so no third party—including us—can access it.
